One Bowl Chocolate Cake

Description

This simple recipe was made by shirleyo on allrecipes. It is quick and simple for anyone to be able to make. In total it only takes one hour to finish and you only have to clean one bowl!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F or 175 degree C. Grease and flour two nine inch round pans
  3. In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
  4. Add the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla, mix for 2 minutes on medium speed of mixer
  5. Stir in the boiling water last. The batter will be thin
  6. Pour evenly into the prepared pans
  7. Bake 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until the cake tests done with a toothpick. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Enjoy
